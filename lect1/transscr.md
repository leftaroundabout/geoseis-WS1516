Stress, strain, elastic moduli
---

For elastic (Hooke) materials:

$$
  \sigma_{ij} = C_{ijkl}\epsilon_{kl}
$$


In the isotropic case
$$
  C_{ijkl} = \lambda_\delta_{ij}\delta_{kl} + \mu(\delta_{ik}\delta_{jl} + \delta_{jk}\delta_{il})
$$
